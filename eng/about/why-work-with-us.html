Facilitate challenge identification & consideration

Explaining one's operational challenges outside one's division can be a challenge unto itself. We can facilitate collaboration and ideation sessions for internal brainstorming and discussion to help flush out and clarify challenges for consideration. In addition, we can seek and collate key information required for challenge consideration, including market research and other other sources of information to evaluate new technology, perform technology scoping and discover new start-ups.

As we refine your challenges, we'll also help select the right program element to address your needs.

Experts in procurement and contracting

We work closely with procurement and canguide you through the procurement and contracing process. For contract solicitation, we'll support you with the research, collection and analysis of information streams and data sources required.

We know the right people

Our partners include the private sector, government and non-government organizations, in addition to the many subject-matter experts within DND, DRDC and the CAF. We can help draw upon their expertise to identify, refine and analyse complex defence challenges.

Project management

We track and manage challenges throughout their entire lifecycle and ensure they stay on track through vendor management and ongoing process meetings.
