Defence innovation & advisory group

The Defence innovation and advisory group (DIAG) is a committee whose purpose is to provide advice and recommendations relevant to establishing and maintaining the strategic direction for the program, to discuss the scientific aspects and content of the IDEaS program and formulate recommendations, directions and decisions to management to comply with science and technology strategic orientation and ensure relevance of delivered solutions to D&S challenges.
