About IDEaS

With a prime focus on helping to strengthen Canada’s defence and security posture mainly through targeted problem solving, the mandate of Director General, Innovation at DRDC encompasses three broad pillars:

  • recruit Canadian innovators from all corners of the Canadian innovation eco-system
  • support innovation
  • help mature innovative ideas into products important to addressing DND/CAF challenges.

This involves:

  • helping to foster a technologically advanced and innovation-driven defence and security sector capable of addressing evolving threats and generating economic benefits
  • recruiting more of the Canadian innovation eco-system to partner in the delivery of S&T for defence and security
  • increasing partnerships and collaboration to foster and build on emerging S&T developed across the innovation eco-system
  • strengthening collaboration and coordination with DRDC science and engineering to help facilitate internally generated innovations important to DND/CAF
  • enhancing Canada’s defence and security posture by delivering technological solutions through two key innovation programs

DG Innovation coordinates CAF/DND participation in Innovation Solutions Canada through the IDEaS program. This program seeks pre-commercial military and defence technologies that have the potential to address current challenges faced by Canada’s Armed Forces. Applicants respond to open competitive innovation calls and are eligible to receive federal government contracts or transfer payments based on their proposed technology plans/prototypes.
