Contests

Contests are a competitive means of finding innovative solutions and awarding prizes to the best solutions. Each contest is a form of open competition amongst innovators and focuses on one of the defence and security challenges identified by the program. Each contest is held independently. The number of winners, as well as the prize money awarded, varies. IDEaS holds approximately four contests per year.
