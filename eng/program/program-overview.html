Program overview

What is IDEaS?

Innovation for Defensive Excellence and Securities (IDEaS) is part of Canada’s new defence policy, Strong, Secure, Engaged, with $1.6 billion committed for investment in innovation for defence and security over the next 20 years.

IDEaS innovation works according to the Solution Readiness Levels (SRL or TRL for Technology), which are a type of measurement system used to assess the maturity level of a particular technology. Each technology project is evaluated against the parameters for each technology level and is then assigned a SRL rating based on the projects' progress.

Program elements

There are 5 program elements that may be used on their own, or together in combination, to address a specific innovation challenge:

Competitive projects

Procurement of research to foster development of promising ideas and solutions

SRL X

Sandboxes

Assess the applicability and effectiveness of prototypes in real-world or simulated conditions

SRL X

Contests

Showcase demonstrable solutions and prototypes through a ranked competition

SRL X

Innovation networks

Establish research clusters and networks of experts focused on innovation in specific themes

SRL X

Test drives

Proof-of-concept testing and validation of new technologies in real environments.
